UP Police SI Admit Card 2026

UP Police Sub Inspector SI Admit Card 2026: Uttar Pradesh Police Recruitment and Promotion Board (UPPRPB) has released the Admit Card on the official website for the recruitment of Sub Inspector and Platoon Commander posts. The exam will be conducted from 14–15 March 2026. Candidates can check their Admit Card using their Enrollment Number / Registration Number / Date of Birth.
